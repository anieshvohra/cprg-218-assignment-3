Shangri-La

About Us

Since 1985, Shangri-La has been a premier tourist resort hotel, offering guests a unique and luxurious escape inspired by the mythical concept of paradise. Known for its exceptional hospitality, breathtaking locations, and world-class amenities, Shangri-La provides a serene retreat where comfort meets elegance. Nestled in some of the world’s most stunning destinations, each resort blends local culture with refined service, creating a tranquil haven for travelers seeking relaxation, indulgence, and unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional hospitality by upholding the core values of integrity, excellence, and sustainability. At Shangri-La, we are committed to creating memorable experiences for every guest by consistently providing warm, personalized service rooted in honesty and respect. We strive for excellence in everything we do—from the quality of our accommodations and dining to the attentiveness of our staff—ensuring that each stay exceeds expectations. Additionally, we prioritize sustainable practices that protect the environment and support local communities, ensuring that our commitment to hospitality goes hand in hand with our responsibility to the world around us.
